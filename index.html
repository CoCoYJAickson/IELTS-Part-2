雅思口语 Part 2 练习工具

你好！这个页面是你的个人口语练习工具。这里没有标准答案，只有帮助你更好、更自信地讲出自己故事的工具。

练习方法：

  1. 选择一个你想练习的话题。
  2. 浏览下方的“词汇库”，熟悉并尝试在回答中使用这些词汇。
  3. 参考“思路引导”，用自己的话，完整地讲述2分钟。
  4. 重复练习，直到你觉得自然、流畅为止。

Describe a difficult challenge you met.

词汇库 (挑战：做游戏)

  • 2D game
  • Unity engine

  • ambitious project
  • steep learning curve
  • coding

  • sound effects

  • frustrating bugs
  • felt like giving up

  • sense of pride

  • problem-solving skills

回答思路引导

  1. 开头：点明挑战"I'd like to talk about a recent challenge I faced, which was creating a 2D game from scratch for my graduation application."
  2. 背景：为什么很难？"It was a huge challenge for me because I had almost no experience in game development. I had to learn everything, from coding in a new language to creating the art and sound for the game."
  3. 过程：你如何应对？"The whole process took about eight long months. There were many times I got stuck on a frustrating bug and felt like giving up. But I persevered by watching online tutorials and asking for help from online forums."
  4. 结果与感受："Finally, I completed the project. Holding the finished game in my hands, I felt an incredible sense of pride. This experience taught me the importance of patience and problem-solving."

  2. 他是什么样的人？(性格/外貌/印象转变)"He's tall, wears glasses, and looks quite muscular. When I first met him, I thought he was shy and a bit clumsy. But as I got to know him, I found that he's actually very caring, reliable, and humorous, especially with his friends."
  3. 一个具体故事："I vividly remember a time during a school excursion in a desert. I was exhausted and ran out of water. Just when I felt I couldn't go on, Jerry came over, shared his own water with me, and kept encouraging me."
  4. 为什么他是你好朋友？"That moment really showed me what a true friend he is. It proved that he's not just a funny guy, but someone you can really count on, especially in difficult times."
